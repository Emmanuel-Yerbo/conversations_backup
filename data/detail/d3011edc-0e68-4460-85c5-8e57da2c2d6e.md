This is a **classic geomorphic mapping conflict**, and there is a clear physical reason for it. 

### Why they cancel each other out:
* **Flooding** happens in low-lying, flat areas (High TWI = 4, but **Low Slope = 1**).

* A flat, flood-prone valley pixel will calculate as: `(Slope 1 * 0.4) + (TWI 4 * 0.4) + (LULC 1 * 0.2) = 2.2` (Class 2: Moderate). 
* As a result, your high flood zones are "diluted" and show up as only moderate risk.

#### Option A: Prioritize Flooding (Recommended for Cape Coast)
